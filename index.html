The Mad Man

Once again, you've played with extreme powers, the likes of which you shouldn't have even known about. And now HE is coming, from a realm of madness far beyond your comprehension. HE comes to destroy space-time itself!

Fortunately, you aren't just a madman - you're also a powerful sorcerer. You know a magic recipe to protect your world from the outer realities in which HE exists, at least for the time being...

Here's how the recipe works:

  • It consist of a list of ingredients you should mix together, adding them to your magic cauldron one by one;
  • Each ingredient is different, but many ingredients have the same magical nature (you know, just like frogs and snake teeth);
  • You should never add two ingredients of the same magical nature one right after the other (you want to protect the world, not destroy it!);
  • Each time you use the recipe, it protects your world for one day;
  • Each time you follow the recipe by mixing the ingredients in some order, that particular order can't be used anymore.
  • Given the list of ingredients for the recipe and their associated magical natures, you need to determine how many days you'll be able to protect your world.
